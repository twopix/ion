ICS (Ioannidis Construction Services) started as a family business in 1959. In 1988, George Ioannidis took over the company, a civil engineer with studies in United States of America specializing in antiseismic constructions. ICS provides holistic construction services, in Greece and around the globe, and is most well-known for its work on houses in the countryside. The company’s portfolio includes more than 100 buildings.
